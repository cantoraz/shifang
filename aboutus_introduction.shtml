Owner

Christine Hesse, born in Innsbruck, studied design management at the University of Westminster in London (MBA). She worked for Kruse Film and DFS&R advertising Munich. Since 1988 Managing Director and joint owner of Hesse Design. Since 1993 lecturer in design management at the Düsseldorf FH. Member of the Design Management Institute of Boston and the Marketing Club Düsseldorf.

Prof. Klaus Hesse, born in Elberfeld in 1954. Studied photography and typography in Wuppertal, worked as art director for advertising agencies such as Ogilvy and GGK Düsseldorf in Hamburg, Munich and Düsseldorf. Since 1988, designer and joint owner of Hesse Design. Chair of communication design in Dortmund and Essen 1993-1999. Currently chair of applied art at the University for Art and Design in Offenbach/Main. Member of the Art Directors Club of Germany and the Type Directors Club of New York.
