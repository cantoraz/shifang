Düsseldorf

Hesse Design Düsseldorf

Hesse Design is located in the nicest part of Duesseldorf's old town between the Academy of Art and the Rhine promenades. The premises of the studios extend in three historical town houses built in 1698. Across the street is the Ursulinen monastery and high school. At Ritterstraße lived no knights but citizens - alike the knights - with tax exemption.
